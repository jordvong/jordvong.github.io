I'm Jordan Vongsithi - a college student interested in the arts and the coffee.

I attend the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth and am going for my BFA in Graphic Design!

Aside from art, my hobbies include listening to lo-fi, playing board games, and having conversations with people.

I am a co-founder of Fishbowl, an open-mic storytelling organization located at the University of Massachusetts Dartmouth. The club is all-inclusive, and is dedicated to providing a platform for everyone to speak and tell their stories.
